Hao Ouyang (欧阳豪)

I am now a Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Dr. Qifeng Chen. Prior to this, I got my Bachelor's degree at CUHK, advised by Prof. Jiaya Jia . I have internship in Google Research, Microsoft Research Asia, Ant Research, Tencent Youtu and Sensetime. My current research interest is image and video generation and editing.
